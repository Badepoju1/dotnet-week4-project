## Brief Reflection
    Through this assignment, I learned how to build a simple console-based Student Management System using C#. I practiced using variables, lists, loops, and conditional statements to store and manage student data.

Overall, the assignment helped strengthen my understanding of basic programming logic and problem-solving in C#.
